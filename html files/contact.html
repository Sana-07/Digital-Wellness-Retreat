Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digital Wellness?
Digital Wellness is a movement to promote balanced use of technology to improve mental and physical health.
How can I participate?
You can participate by enrolling in our programs, attending our events, and following our guidelines for digital wellness.
What are the benefits?
Benefits include improved mental health, reduced stress, better sleep, and enhanced productivity.
Who can join?
Our programs are open to everyone looking to improve their digital habits and overall wellness.
Where are you located?
We are located at various locations across the country. Check our website for the nearest center.
How can I contact you?
You can contact us through our website, email, or by visiting our main office.
