Our History

Founded in 2020, the Digital Wellness Retreat began as a small initiative to help individuals manage their digital lives more effectively. Recognizing the increasing impact of technology on mental health, our founders, Jane and John Doe, envisioned a place where people could find balance in a tech-saturated world. Starting with a few online mindfulness classes, the retreat quickly grew into a comprehensive wellness platform, offering a variety of programs designed to enhance overall well-being.
